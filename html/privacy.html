Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Drop Watcher, operated at instockornot.club, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web monitoring and alert service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide when signing up for alerts:

  • Email address — to send you drop alerts and service communications
  • Phone number (optional) — to send SMS alerts, only if you explicitly opt in
  • Watch preferences — the URLs and keywords you want to track
  • Name (optional) — to personalize your alerts

We do not collect payment information, physical addresses, browsing history, or any other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Send you email or SMS alerts when keywords matching your watch appear on your tracked page
  • Manage your subscription and preferences
  • Prevent abuse of the service (rate limiting)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties. We do not send marketing messages. All communications are transactional alerts based on your explicit watch configuration.

3. Email Communications

You can unsubscribe from email alerts at any time by:

  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in any alert email
  • Using the STOP button on your personal dashboard
  • Contacting support@instockornot.club

4. Data Retention

We retain your contact information and preferences for as long as your subscription is active. Alert history (drops detected) is retained for 30 days. You may request deletion of all your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Security

Subscriber data is stored on a secured server with restricted file permissions. Secrets and configuration are separated from public-facing code. We use TLS encryption for all connections. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Drop Watcher:

  • Resend — email delivery
  • Twilio — SMS delivery
  • Cloudflare — DNS and CDN
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI-powered page analysis

These services process data in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable law. We do not share your personal data with these services beyond what is necessary to deliver the service (e.g., your email address to Resend to send you an alert).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For questions, data requests, or support: support@instockornot.club

SMS Terms & Conditions

By opting in to SMS alerts from Drop Watcher, you agree to the following terms.

Program Name

Drop Watcher SMS Alerts

Service Description

Drop Watcher is a free web monitoring service. When you opt in to SMS, you will receive text message alerts when keywords you specified are detected on a webpage you are watching. Messages are transactional alerts only — never marketing, promotions, or advertising.

Opt-In

You opt in to SMS alerts by entering your phone number on instockornot.club and checking the SMS consent checkbox during signup. By checking that box, you consent to receive automated text messages from Drop Watcher at the phone number you provided.

You are not required to opt in to SMS as a condition of using the service. Email alerts are available without SMS.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on keyword matches on your watched pages. You may receive anywhere from zero to several messages per day depending on activity. Typical usage is a few messages per week.

Msg & data rates may apply. Check with your carrier for details on your messaging plan.

Opt-Out

You can stop receiving SMS alerts at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message from Drop Watcher
  • Using the STOP button on your personal dashboard at instockornot.club
  • Contacting support@instockornot.club

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message. You will not receive any further SMS from us unless you opt in again.

Help

For help with SMS alerts:

  • Reply HELP to any message from Drop Watcher
  • Email support@instockornot.club

Privacy

Your phone number and SMS opt-in consent are not sold, rented, or shared with any third parties for marketing or any other purpose. Your phone number is shared only with our SMS provider (Twilio) for the sole purpose of delivering your alerts.

Supported Carriers

SMS alerts are available on all major US carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, and most regional carriers. Carrier support may vary.
